Unique Partner Email

Maintain a clean database by preventing duplicate contacts

Why use this module?

In a standard Odoo installation, multiple partners can share the same email address. This often leads to:

  • Duplicate CRM leads and customers.
  • Confusion during email marketing campaigns.
  • Inaccurate reporting and customer history.

Key Features

Real-time Alerts

Users get an immediate warning if they enter an existing email.

Clean CRM

Significantly reduces the need for manual record merging.
