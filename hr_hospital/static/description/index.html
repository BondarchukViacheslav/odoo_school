Odoo School Hospital

Comprehensive Hospital Management System

This module provides a robust framework for managing hospital operations, including patient records, doctor schedules, and medical visits.

Key Features

Doctor Management

Track doctor specialties, licenses, and experience. Manage mentorship for interns and work schedules.

Patient Records

Maintain detailed patient cards, including blood groups, medical history, and emergency contacts.

Visit Scheduling

Plan and track patient visits. Includes automatic allergy warnings and constraints to prevent double-booking.

Diagnosis & Reporting

Record multiple diagnoses per visit and generate detailed medical reports using built-in wizards.
